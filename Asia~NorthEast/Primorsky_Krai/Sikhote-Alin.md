# [[Sikhote-Alin]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Sikhote-Alin,155322|WD~Sikhote-Alin,155322]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Sikhote-Alin** (, SEE-kə-TAY ə-LEEN; Russian: Сихотэ́-Али́нь [sʲɪxɐˈtɛ ɐˈlʲinʲ]) 
> is a mountain range in [[../Primorsky_Krai|Primorsky_Krai]] and [[../Khabarovsk_Krai|Khabarovsk_Krai]], Russia, 
> extending about 900 kilometres (560 mi) to the northeast of the Russian Pacific seaport of Vladivostok. 
> 
> The highest summits are Tordoki Yani at 2,077 metres (6,814 ft) above sea level, 
> Ko Mountain (2,003 metres (6,572 ft)) in Khabarovsk Krai 
> and Anik Mountain (1,933 metres (6,342 ft)) in Primorsky Krai.
>
